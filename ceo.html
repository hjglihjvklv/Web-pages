Meet Our CEO

Founder & Visionary Leader

As the founder and CEO of GreenCart, I've dedicated my career to creating sustainable solutions that benefit both people and the planet. What started as a small idea has grown into a movement that's changing how we think about everyday products.

With over 15 years of experience in sustainable business practices, I've made it my mission to prove that eco-friendly products can be both high-quality and accessible to everyone. Our company's success is built on three core principles: sustainability, innovation, and community.

When I'm not working on growing GreenCart, you can find me speaking at sustainability conferences, mentoring young entrepreneurs, or enjoying nature with my family.
